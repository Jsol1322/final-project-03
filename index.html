MARATHON

A New Extraction Shooter by Bungie

Overview

Marathon reimagines Bungie's classic sci-fi universe as a competitive extraction shooter. Players explore hostile environments, collect valuable loot, and attempt to extract safely while competing against other players and AI threats.
